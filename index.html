Why this retreat?

This retreat offers a unique opportunity to disconnect from the hustle of everyday life and reconnect with yourself in a tranquil setting. With a focus on holistic wellness, you'll engage in activities like Yoga, Meditation, Sound Therapy, and Cacao Ceremony that nurture your mind, body and spirit.

Surrounded by the beauty of La Lande-de-Fronsac, this charming village, nestled in the heart of Bordeaux, wine country, is renowned for its picturesque vineyards and stunning landscapes. You'll not only rejuvenate but also immerse yourself in rich local culture, creating lasting memories and genuine connection.

A Journey Like No Other: Yoga, Sound & Culture in a French Château


I'm Ruchika Sadh, founder of Ida Yoga & Wellness and owner of a yoga studio in New Delhi. My passion is to bring a holistic approach to peaceful and happy living through yoga, Pilates, meditation, breathwork, sound therapy, and hypnotherapy. After years of studying and practicing across the world, I invite you to experience something truly unique—an immersive retreat blending the sacred wisdom of India with the elegance of French culture.

Set in a breathtaking 1800s château, surrounded by lush vineyards and open countryside, this retreat is a fusion of tradition and indulgence. You'll practice authentic Indian yoga and meditation, while embracing the slowness and richness of French living—a rare combination that makes this retreat one of a kind.

Why This Retreat?

A Journey of Deep Healing & Transformation

This isn't just a yoga retreat—it's a reset for your mind, body, and soul. In today's fast-paced world, we rarely get the chance to pause, breathe, and truly listen to ourselves. This retreat is designed to help you release stress, detox emotionally, and reconnect with yourself through ancient healing practices and immersive experiences.

Candle Meditation

A simple yet powerful practice to enhance focus, strengthen eyesight, and calm the nervous system through mental clarity & eye health techniques.

Sound Therapy & Hypnosis

Let vibrations guide you into deep relaxation, helping you release emotional blockages through subconscious healing practices.

Traditional Pranayama

Experience rare breathwork techniques designed to purify the system and balance energy through our specialized breathwork detox program.

Chakra Healing

Align your energy centers to feel lighter, centered, and more at peace through emotional balance practices.

Nature Immersion

Move beyond the mat with yoga on a boat, vineyard bike tours & nature therapy to experience the healing power of nature.

Cultural Experience

Discover the joy of mindful, nourishing eating through French cooking classes in the heart of the countryside.

Rooted in authentic yogic traditions and Ayurveda, I bring years of experience in guiding people toward holistic well-being. During the retreat, I also offer private consultations and personalized sessions, helping you create a customized wellness plan suited to your needs.

If you're seeking true relaxation, emotional healing, and a deeper mind-body connection, this retreat is for you.
